Typhoon Saloon

Typhoon Saloon is Pacifc Beach's premier & largest nightclub & sports bar only 4 blocks from the beach centrally located on PB's busiest strand, Garnet. It is the ultimate atmosphere for casual clubbing for those who are 21+. Offering impressive top of the line speaker & lighting systems to amplify your experience and help any celebration you host playing an array of top 40 & dance music.
